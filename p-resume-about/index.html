About Me


I am an artist currently studying at University of California, Santa Cruz. I am a aspiring animator with knowledge of 2D frame animation, rotoscope animation, as well as 3D animation. I also paint using mediums of acryclic, watercolor, gouache, and digital. I am experienced using adobe porgrams as well as 3D programs like Maya and Blender. My goal is to tell stories through my work that will connect with poeple. I would like to showcase work that deals with challenges and ways to process them. I also want to inspire others to go on adventures and create their own stories when life seems a little boring.
